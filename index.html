Aangan — Privacy Policy

Effective date: 17 June 2026 Last updated: 17 June 2026 App: Aangan (आँगन) · bundle com.kunba.aangan Publisher: Kunba Contact: Ateeshay.jain@gmail.com

The short version. Aangan is a local-first app. Your household data —
staff, salaries, bills, expenses, documents, photos — is stored **on your own
iPhone. We run no servers and we do not collect, transmit, sell, or
share your data. There is no analytics, tracking, or advertising** of any
kind. Nothing leaves your device unless you explicitly turn on iCloud sync,
share a home with someone you invite, or use an optional feature described
below. This is verifiable in the app's behaviour and source.

1. Who we are

Aangan is published by Kunba. For privacy questions, contact Ateeshay.jain@gmail.com. Because all personal data stays on your device and under your control, you are effectively the custodian of your own data; Kunba does not hold or process it on your behalf on any server.

2. What data the app stores (all on your device)

Aangan helps you run a household, so you can enter information such as:

  • Household: home names, members, family/housemate details.
  • People & staff: names, roles, phone numbers, salaries, attendance, advances and payments, and — if you choose to add them — identity documents (e.g. Aadhaar, PAN) and their photos.
  • Money: bills, subscriptions, expenses, payment method (cash/UPI), counters (e.g. milk), UPI/IFSC details you enter for payments.
  • Property: assets, warranties, insurance, vehicle papers, and document images/PDFs you scan or attach.
  • Home life: meals, contacts, to-dos, calendar events, laundry, and shared expense splits/settlements.
  • Attachments: photos and PDFs you add (receipts, bills, warranty cards, documents), stored in the app's private storage on the device.

Where it lives. All of the above is stored in a local database on your iPhone. Kunba operates no backend for your household data and never receives it. The ONE exception is the optional staff verification service, which uses a Kunba-operated backend and is described in full in §6.

3. Sensitive information

Some of what you may enter is sensitive — government IDs (Aadhaar/PAN), financial details (salaries, UPI, bank IFSC), and document images. Aangan treats this with care:

  • It is stored only on your device (and, if you enable it, your own private iCloud — see §5).
  • The Property area (where IDs and documents live) can be protected behind Face ID / device passcode.
  • The device's storage is protected by iOS data-protection (encryption at rest) tied to your passcode.
  • You are never required to add a government ID; those fields are optional.

4. What we do NOT do

  • We do not collect or transmit your household data to any Kunba server. (The optional staff-verification service, §6, is the sole feature that talks to a Kunba-operated backend — and only when you use it.)
  • We do not use analytics, telemetry, crash-reporting, advertising, or tracking SDKs. The app does not build a profile of you and does not track you across apps or websites.
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties.

5. iCloud & home sharing (optional, your own account)

If you enable iCloud sync and/or share a home, Aangan uses Apple iCloud (CloudKit) to store and sync that home's data:

  • Sync uses your own iCloud account. Data is processed by Apple under Apple's iCloud terms and privacy policy — not by Kunba.
  • When you share a home, that home's data is made available only to the specific people you invite (via Apple's secure sharing). You control who is invited and can stop sharing.
  • If you do not enable iCloud or sharing, your data stays solely on your device.

6. Staff verification (optional, uses a Kunba backend)

If you choose to run a background verification of a staff member, Aangan uses a backend operated by Kunba (Supabase, hosted in Mumbai, India) and regulated payment/verification partners. This is the only feature that sends data to Kunba, and nothing is sent until the staff member consents in-app and you proceed.

Kunba is the data fiduciary for this service under India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (you, running your household, generally enjoy the personal-purpose exemption; Kunba, operating the service, does not).

Refunds: if a paid request cannot be run (e.g. details lapsed before submission), it is flagged for refund rather than silently dropped.

7. Optional cloud AI assistance (off by default)

Aangan includes an optional feature to help parse a diet chart using a cloud AI service. It is disabled by default and not configured out of the box:

  • It does nothing unless you (a) enter an HTTPS proxy URL in Settings → Integrations and (b) explicitly enable the feature flag. Insecure (non-HTTPS) URLs are rejected.
  • When used, only the diet-chart text is sent — and known identifiers (Aadhaar, PAN, phone numbers, IFSC) are redacted on your device before sending.
  • You choose the endpoint it talks to. Kunba does not operate or receive this data. Review that provider's privacy terms before enabling.
  • Until configured, the feature returns nothing and sends nothing.

Aangan requests permissions only for features you use; each maps to a real feature, and you can decline or revoke any of them in iOS Settings:

9. Features that hand off to other apps (you initiate)

Some actions open another app or website at your request — these are normal iOS hand-offs, not background data sharing:

  • Share a salary slip / PDF via the iOS share sheet (e.g. WhatsApp) — only when you tap share.
  • Warranty registration opens the manufacturer's website in your browser; no data is sent automatically.
  • UPI payments / WhatsApp / phone open the relevant app via standard links.

10. Children

Aangan is intended for adults managing a household. It is not directed at children and does not knowingly collect data from children.

11. Data retention & deletion

  • Your data persists on your device until you delete it (delete records in the app, or delete the app to remove its local store).
  • If you used iCloud sync, removing data and/or signing out of iCloud removes the synced copy from your iCloud per Apple's process.
  • Shared-home participants you remove lose access going forward.
  • Verification data: transient identifiers are deleted at submission (or within 24 h if unpaid); the report and consent record persist until you delete the verification (in-app, per verification or all at once — §6) or request deletion.
  • Export: Settings → "Export household data" gives you a portable copy of a home's data (spreadsheets + JSON) at any time — your right to data portability, self-served.

12. Your rights

Because the data lives on your device under your control, you can access, correct, export (e.g. share as PDF), and delete it directly in the app at any time. For questions about these rights, contact Ateeshay.jain@gmail.com. We aim to honour applicable rights under India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 and comparable laws; in practice, you exercise most of them yourself within the app.

13. Security

Data is stored using iOS's protected local storage (encryption at rest tied to your device passcode). Sensitive areas can be gated behind Face ID. Optional cloud calls are HTTPS-only with on-device PII redaction. No security measure is perfect; keep your device passcode and iCloud account secure.

14. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the app evolves. Material changes will be reflected by an updated "Last updated" date and, where appropriate, an in-app notice.
