1. What Aangan is

Aangan (आँगन) is a household-management app for Indian families: staff attendance and salary, daily deliveries, bills, expenses, documents, and related tools. Your household data lives on your device and, if you enable sync or sharing, in your own Apple iCloud account — we do not operate a server for it.
